MICTLAN SURF SHOP


Our shop has a huge selection of over 60 boards for rent from shortboards and guns to high performance longboards and even soft tops. With a full range of shapes and sizes, you can afford to travel light and save airline fees. Mictlan stock of surf accessories and Mictlan Ts assure that you are covered in the water and out.

On site repair is available to remedy everything from dings to busted boards, profesional repair jobs keep your stick in running order.

We are located in front of Burros Surf Spot.
