Mictlan's Staff and Fleet

21 July 2011
Published in Mictlan

Mictlan is Punta de Mita's premier surf company with several professional ISA certified surf and SUP instructors with years of surf experience.  Mictlan is a full serivce surf operator offering boat tours to the hottest breaks in town, surf and SUP lessons for any age and level, equipment rentals and sales, and even surfboard shapes and repairs.  At the shop, a full stock of over 60 boards from 5'5" to 11' are available for rent as well as rash guards, wetsuits, wax, and other surf accesories.  The aquatic fleet of the company is comprised of 4 boats equiped for surfing, fishing, and other marine activities.

Surf Lessons and Clinics

02 June 2011
Published in Surfing

Our goal is to create a fun introduction to the basics of surfing that is accessible to each new surfer, inabling them to stand up and ride several waves, ultimately cultivating an ability to continue this beautiful sport.  For beginners, a 20 minute briefing is followed by 2 hours of in-water time at one of the local, gental breaks.  Once comfortable standing and riding the waves, new surfers are ready for intermediate surf clinics based mainly on supervision and coaching by our professional instructors, working to improve technique and self sufficiency in slightly larger and stronger surf.
