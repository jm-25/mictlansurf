Scuba Diving

Live and enjoy Banderas bay from another perspective, underwater!

If you are a certified diver enjoy the aquatic biodiversity of the Marietas islands with a great number of colorful fish as well as eels, octopuses and manta rays. Get inside your underwater caves and spend an excellent time in direct contact with the natural environment.

If you are an adventurer who wants to know and experience the underwater world, join us in this new adventure alongside a PADI certified instructor in the Discover Scuba Diving program and know what it is to breed underwater.

If you are a person who loves aquatic life, choose your PADI Open Water Diver certification and still enjoy the marine ecosystem around the world.

Banderas Bay is one of the largest bays in the world which allows divers to have more than 10 options of dive points for different level or degree of experience.

All our diving trips are private, with the best equipment, always accompanied by a professional certified in diving.
