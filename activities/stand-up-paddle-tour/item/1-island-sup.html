Island SUP

Get to know the Marietas Islands Reserve in a completly new, intimate way as you paddle through and around the caves and volcanic formations of the islands.  Exchange glances with the bizarre looking blue-footed boobies, who may find you to be an equally new and strange site as well.  Our ISA certified paddle board instructors will introduce you to the techniques and benefits of this new core strength building sport as well as to the Islands and their exotic marine and bird life.
