Sports Fishing

A full day of fishing offers opportunities for catches of yellow fin tuna, blue, black, and striped marlin, sailfish, mahi-mahi, wahoo, and rainbow runners; While a relaxing trip of inshore fishing offers the possibility for catches of rooster fish, spanish mackerel, pacific dog-tooth snapper, almaco jack and more.

Our custom fishing panga is equipped with tuna tubes and full Penn International fishing gear, and is stocked with live bait and a cooler with refreshments.

Contact us to find out about the latest fish action and plan your trip.
